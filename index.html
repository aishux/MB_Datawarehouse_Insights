Key Country Observations

🏆 UAE is the #1 market by every measure: 126,558 accounts (13% of total), 25% funded rate (above average), $36.4M NMI (52% of all 2026 NMI), and the highest GA traffic at 397k sessions.
🇨🇦 Canada punches above its weight: 31.8% funded rate — the highest of any major market — and $8.3M NMI. High-value, high-intent audience.
🇮🇹 Italy has exceptional conversion: 33.5% funded rate (best of large markets) despite only $395 average account size. Volume conversion machine.
⚠️ USA is a major red flag: 17,775 accounts but only 426 funded = 2.4% conversion. The worst funded rate by far. Possible regulatory restrictions, wrong audience targeting, or product-market fit issues.
⚠️ Negative NMI in several markets: France (−$326K), UK (−$1.16M), Mexico (−$1.13M), Italy (−$2.92M), Pakistan (−$547K), Vietnam (−$84K). These markets are cash-negative in 2026 YTD — may be new or promotional.
🇲🇽 Mexico bounce rate 86.6%: Abnormally high. Almost 9 in 10 sessions bounce. This suggests a serious landing page relevance or localisation problem for Mexican traffic.

How the Analysis Was Built — Step by Step

This section explains the technical decisions made to arrive at the core KPIs. It's useful context for anyone trying to reproduce, extend, or QA the numbers.
Step 1 — Map the warehouse

Listed all candidate tables and pulled their schemas using INFORMATION_SCHEMA.COLUMNS. Identified columns containing the right signals: has_ftd, Account_Type, NMI, total_deposit_usd, demo flags, GA Views, Bounce_rate.

Step 2 — Probe categorical fields

Ran distinct-value queries on Account_Type (Individual Client / IB / Demo), Funded flag, and demo flags in dm_profile. This confirmed which flag means "funded" (has_ftd = 1) and which means "live" (live_acct_flag = 1).

Step 3 — Discover the country key problem

Found that accounts use ISO2, accounts_daily uses full country names, GA uses ISO2, and paidmarketing uses marketing region labels. These are incompatible — a direct join would silently drop or duplicate data.

Step 4 — Decide join strategy

Standardised account/GA/daily tables to ISO2 using lkp_country and country_mapping lookup tables. Marketing geo was left on its own axis — regional labels like "Worldwide" and "MENA" cannot be mapped to individual countries. Result: cross-source KPIs (CPA, CPFA, ROI, LPV) are reported at total level only. Country-level breakdowns exist only for account + GA metrics.

Step 5 — Resolve the deposit source conflict

Discovered that accounts_python.total_deposits_usd sums to $237B — inflated by multi-account duplication (same user, multiple accounts). Switched to dm_profile.total_deposit_usd which is deduplicated per portal user, totalling $2.32B. Avg account size = $15,074.

Step 6 — Run total aggregates

Executed 6 SQL queries against the live warehouse. Q1: account counts/funded. Q2: marketing cost + traffic + NMI + GA. Q3: demo/live/deposits from dm_profile. Q4: gross trading revenue from dm_bv_revenue. Q5: merged by-country (ISO2 spine). Q6: marketing CTR/CPC by geo.

Step 7 — Export to Excel

Results exported to dw_mb_kpis.xlsx with sheets for KPI Summary (Actual vs Target), By Country, and Definitions & Sources. Built using openpyxl.

SQL Queries Used

Q1. Account Counts & Funded Drives KPIs 2, 3, 5
SELECT COUNT(*) total_accts,
  SUM(CASE WHEN Account_Type IN ('Individual Client','Introducing Broker') THEN 1 ELSE 0 END) client_accts,
  SUM(has_ftd) funded_accts
FROM accounts_python WHERE is_test=0;
Q2. Marketing Cost + Traffic + NMI + GA Bounce Drives KPIs 1, 7, 8, 9, 10, 11
SELECT
  (SELECT SUM(Cost) FROM paidmarketing_2024)                          paid_cost,
  (SELECT SUM(Clicks) FROM paidmarketing_2024)                        paid_clicks,
  (SELECT SUM(Impressions) FROM paidmarketing_2024)                   paid_impr,
  (SELECT SUM(Cost) FROM adjust)                                      adjust_cost,
  (SELECT SUM(spend) FROM stg_offline_marketing_spend
     WHERE date <= '2026-06-24')                                      offline_actual,
  (SELECT SUM(NMI) FROM accounts_daily_2026)                          nmi_2026,
  (SELECT SUM(Views) FROM stg_ga_csv)                                 ga_views,
  (SELECT SUM(Views*Bounce_rate)/SUM(Views) FROM stg_ga_csv
     WHERE Views > 0)                                                 ga_bounce_wtd;
Q3. Demo / Live / Deposits from dm_profile Drives KPIs 4 & 6
SELECT COUNT(*) users,
  SUM(live_acct_flag)                                                  live_users,
  SUM(CASE WHEN total_deposit_usd > 0 THEN 1 ELSE 0 END)              funded_users,
  SUM(total_deposit_usd)                                               total_deposit,
  SUM(total_deposit_usd) /
    NULLIF(SUM(CASE WHEN total_deposit_usd > 0 THEN 1 ELSE 0 END),0)  avg_funded_acct_size,
  SUM(pure_demo_flag) pure_demo, SUM(demo_to_live_flag) demo_to_live
FROM dm_profile;
Q4. Gross Trading Revenue ROI numerator
SELECT SUM(total_usd_spreads)   spreads,
       SUM(total_usd_commission) commission,
       SUM(total_usd_swaps)      swaps,
       SUM(total_usd_spreads + total_usd_commission + total_usd_swaps) gross_revenue
FROM dm_bv_revenue;
